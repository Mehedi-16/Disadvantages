Disadvantages of Our University

Academic Sector

  • Lack of updated curriculum
  • Insufficient faculty resources
  • Limited research opportunities

Infrastructure Sector

  • Poor maintenance of buildings and facilities
  • Outdated technology and equipment
  • Limited space in libraries and study areas

Administrative Sector

  • Slow processing of paperwork
  • Inadequate student support services
  • Lack of transparency in decision-making

Financial Sector

  • High tuition fees
  • Limited availability of scholarships and financial aid
  • Poor financial management and allocation of resources

Social Sector

  • Limited extracurricular activities
  • Lack of diversity and inclusivity
  • Weak student community and engagement
